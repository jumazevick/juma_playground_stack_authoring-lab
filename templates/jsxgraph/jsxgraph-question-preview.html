Quick self-report (9-point manikin)

Survey-only preview with a reusable manikin scale and question text.

Difficulty
How difficult did this problem feel to understand and solve, considering the way it was presented?
Effort
How much mental effort did you invest in interpreting the problem and working toward your solution?
Pleasure
How pleasant or unpleasant was your experience of solving this version of the problem?
Arousal
While solving this version, how calm, mentally activated, or tense did you feel?
Dominance
While solving this version, to what extent did you feel in control of what to do next rather than overwhelmed?
